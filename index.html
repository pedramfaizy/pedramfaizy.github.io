Introduction and Description

Discover Awazxane:

Preserving Kurdish musical heritage

Awazxane is a dedicated digital archive for preserving and sharing Kurdish music and neighboring cultural traditions. As a comprehensive note-sheet bank, it serves artists, educators, students, and researchers with easy access to authentic compositions and folk melodies.

Dive into a world of musical treasures, where traditional vocal and instrumental works are meticulously documented for exploration.

Invitation and Overview

Contribute to Awazxane:

Share your musical contributions

Awazxane invites musicians, composers, and enthusiasts to contribute to our archive by submitting personal notations of Kurdish compositions or folk melodies from related traditions.

This collaborative space encourages community-driven growth, allowing shared works to inspire both preservation and innovation. By connecting contributions with a global audience dedicated to cultural heritage, we aim to foster a rich exchange of ideas and creativity.
