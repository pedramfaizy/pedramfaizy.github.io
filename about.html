Introduction and Description

Discover Awazxane:
Preserving Kurdish musical heritage


Awazxane is a dedicated digital archive for preserving and sharing Kurdish music and neighboring cultural traditions. As a comprehensive note-sheet bank, it serves artists, educators, students, and researchers with easy access to authentic compositions and folk melodies.

Dive into a world of musical treasures, where traditional vocal and instrumental works are meticulously documented for exploration.
